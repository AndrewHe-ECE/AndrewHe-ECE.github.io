Salinity Detector: Completed May 2022    

One of the largest K-12 STEM competitions in the world with over 8,000 teams in fifty states. Medalists in events at State, and National levels. In 2022 Science Olympiad, I won State Champion and National 2nd place for Detector Building, an electrical engineering event of building a microcontroller-based water salinity detector, among other awards 

 - Salinity Detector (accurate to 0.5ppm) - Discovered water capacitance to the issue of the detector variance in measured resistance and rectified the issue with reed relays to discharge the capacitance between cycles 

- 2nd at Nationals and 1st at States
