About Me

As a Michigander from the Detroit metropolitan area, I am studying at Princeton University towards a Bachelor of Science in Engineering with a major in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

I am passionate about electrical engineering. Whether for competitions, research, or just for the fun of it, I enjoy working with things hands-on. Tinkering with microcontrollers, testing terahertz radio systems, or creating algorithms in machine learning, I love working on projects. Here below are some of my projects for your reference.

View Projects

 

In terms of current employment, I am involved with a part-time research internship at the Smart Wireless Agile Networks (SWAN) Lab. I am working on projects in this lab involving terahertz radio systems. In addition, I am also involved with the Inks and Interfaces Lab, where we develop the next generation of battery technologies. Through this lab, I analyze X-Rays of current battery materials and develop solutions to increase the density and performance of battery technology.

On campus, I'm involved in the business department of the Daily Princetonian, the country's second-oldest daily college newspaper. Tasks include managing the operation budget, advertising, and recruiting events. In addition, I am part of Princeton Racing Electric (PRE), a student organization dedicated to designing, building, and racing high-performance electric vehicles, where I am part of the tractive systems team.
